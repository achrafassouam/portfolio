My Personal Bio

Assouam Achraf is a foreign trade manager at Hatch, a home and kitchen appliances company, he is passionate about becoming a software engineer and he’s always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow.

Two of Assouam’s biggest achievements while working at Hatch are developing a new inventory management system that improved efficiency and accuracy and also helping in changing the packaging design for half of the company’s products, which resulted in 10% increase in sales. He was awarded the company’s employee of the year award.

His goal is to become a software engineer, he is looking forward to the challenges he might face while learning new technologies and he is passionate about making a change in this world with his work.
